How do I use slices?
What is a slice?
It's an array which is formed as a subsequence of an existing array.
The purpose is for performing in-place mutation according to a bounds smaller
than the containing array.

How would I do a quicksort partition in my array?

(let (partition (function (l)
      (let (a (slice l (from 0 to ((# l) / 2))))
           (b (slice l (from ((# l) / 2) to (# l))))
           (list a b))))
 (partition list)
